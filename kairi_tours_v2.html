Our Story

Kenya's Oldest Indigenous Tour Agency

Founded in 1984, Kairi Tours & Safaris is proudly Kenya's oldest indigenously owned tour company. We are the only tour agency with an office at JKIA Arrivals Terminal 1A — open around the clock, ready to welcome you the moment your plane lands.

Over four decades, we've built unshakeable relationships with lodges, conservancies, and guides across Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and beyond — giving you insider access and competitive rates that no newcomer can match.

Expert multilingual local guides — deep knowledge of wildlife, culture, and hidden gems across East Africa
Tailor-made itineraries — budget, mid-range and luxury packages crafted around your schedule and interests
Sustainable tourism — eco-conscious travel that supports local communities and conservation
